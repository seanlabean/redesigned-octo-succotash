What is an esolang?

An esoteric language is a collection of logically consistent syntax and modes of expression with little to no intended purpose or a purpose so incredibly niche that its usefulness is constrained to the most unique of projects. An esolang that I'm sure nearly everyone is aware of, whether they've realized it or not, is Tengwar — one of Tolkien's Elvish languages and associated script.

But what I am interested in this post is the creation and use of esoteric languages of the coding variety.
