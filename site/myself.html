Welcome. My name is Sean Lewis. I am originally from Sunnyvale, California and moved to Philadelphia in 2017 to pursue my dream of being an astrophysicist. In addition to my love of astronomy, I enjoy reading and computer science.

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at Drexel University where my research focuses on computational astrophysics and the modeling of star cluster formation and developing a novel software tool to translate data from different astrophysical simulation code bases. I received my Masters degree in physics from Drexel Univeristy in 2019. Prior to graduate school, I received my B.S. in physics at California Polytechnic State University — San Luis Obispo in 2016.
